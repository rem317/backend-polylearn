Learning objectives and examples

Learning Objectives

  • Define polynomials and identify their components
  • Classify polynomials by degree and number of terms
  • Evaluate polynomials for given values
  • Add and subtract polynomial expressions
  • Apply polynomials to real-world problems

Example:

3x² + 2x - 5

This polynomial has three terms: 3x² (quadratic term), 2x (linear term), and -5 (constant term)
