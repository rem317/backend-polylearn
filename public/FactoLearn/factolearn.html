Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your inbox.

How can I track my learning progress?

Visit the Progress page in your dashboard. You'll find detailed analytics about your completed lessons, practice exercises, and quiz scores.

Are there mobile apps available?

Yes! MathHub is available on both iOS and Android. Download from the App Store or Google Play Store.

How do I earn badges and points?

Complete lessons, practice exercises, and participate in quiz games to earn points. Badges are awarded for achieving specific milestones.
